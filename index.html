  • Short history of club
    Tennis Club was founded in 1923 by the Ladies and Gentlemen of the Anytown Rotary Association. It was orgininally sited on fields behind the high street, before moving to it's current location off Church Avenue in 1950. Club competitions have been held for Ladies and Gentlemen every year, excepting during the hostilites of WW2. A full calendar of social events is also held at the club's fine facilities. New members are always welcome.
